Research

I'm interested in various weakly supervised learning problems in the context of computer vision, including completely unlabelled self-supervised learning, semi-supervised learning with partial data having labels, and learning with noisy/coarse labels. I'm happy to discuss any related topics and possible collaborations, please simply drop me an email!
