Lecturer (Asst. Prof.) @ QUB 讲师 (助理教授) @ QUB

I am a Lecturer in Machine Learning at Queen's University Belfast. My research centers on building robust, reliable, and secure AI systems. Prior to joining QUB, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the IIML Group at UCL, collaborating with Prof. Miguel Rodrigues. I received my Ph.D. from QMUL (supervised by Prof. Ioannis Patras), and spent time as an AI4Media Junior Exchange Fellow at the University of Trento, working with Prof. Nicu Sebe. I hold a Master's from Tsinghua University (supervised by Prof. Jin Gu), and a Bachelor's from Nankai University.

On a personal note, I grew up in Chizhou, Anhui Province, China—a beautiful small city located near the renowned scenic area of Huangshan (Yellow Mountain).
